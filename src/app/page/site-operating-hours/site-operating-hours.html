Note:  This report is provided to assist you in counseling discussions with your Station Managers and Retailers regarding hours of operation. The report reflects the time of the first and last credit card transactions for each station in your network, by day, for the prior week. A summary column lists the average number of hours each site was open daily for the week. This report will help you quickly identify stations running short hours. With the exception of car washes, or sites where not allowed by law, all Chevron and Texaco locations are encouraged to be open a minimum of 16 hours per day.
